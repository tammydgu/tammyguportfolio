PROJECT OVERVIEW

Humm is a company building a consumer-grade wearable device, backed by leading neuroscience conducted at UCSF, that gently stimulates the prefrontal cortext to improve an individual’s working memory by 20%. In early 2019, Humm introduced a company rebrand better tailored to their older professionals and retirees target market. A critical component of the company rebrand required redesigning the Humm website to be consistent with and embody a brand that was less tech focused and more lifestyle focused - a premium brand for people passionate about growth, learning, and health.
