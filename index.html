Humm (2019)

Humm

UI/UX, User Research, Web Design

Humm is a neuroscience company building a consumer-grade wearable device that improves working memory by 20%. In 2019, Humm commissioned a complete company rebrand and website redesign to better appeal to their new, highest potential target market.
Pao: Smart Home Tea Kettle (2019)

Pao: Smart Home Tea Kettle

UI/UX, Product Design

Pao is a smart tea kettle that seeks to increase user flexbility with regards to when, where, and for what purpose they may use their water devices, while continuing to be inspired by, respect, and supplement the beauty of traditional tea ceremonies.
WordPress (2019)

WordPress

Information Architecture Case Study

This case study examined the efficiency and accessibility of the global navigation on the WordPress website. Through multiple tree tests and card sorts, this project focused on a global navigation redesign that aimed for more user-centric, intentional, and specific terminology.
Declutter (2019)

Declutter

UI/UX, User Research

Declutter is a mobile app that seeks to improve a user's experience of falling asleep by allowing them to more intuitively track their sleep cycles and better organize their thoughts.
HTC Vive vs. Oculus Rift (2019)

HTC Vive vs. Facebook Oculus Rift

VR, Content Strategy Case Study

The primary objective of this case study was to conduct an in-depth analysis of both the HTC Vive and Oculus Rift websites. A thorough content audit of these two sites were completed with the intention of providing suggestions towards more readable and user-centric content.
