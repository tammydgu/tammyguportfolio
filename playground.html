"The world is but a canvas to the imagination." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

I have always understood the word “play” as referring to a desire to interact with one’s environment - a word characterized by curiosity, childlike wonder, and joy. It fuels creativity, drives innovation, and inspires art. From sketchpads to tablets to fingertips on frosty car windows, I am constantly finding ways to connect with the world around me. Welcome to my playground!
